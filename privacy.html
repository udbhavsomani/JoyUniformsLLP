Privacy Policy

Deployment note: This practical draft should be reviewed by your legal adviser before publication, particularly after adding analytics, advertising pixels or other third-party services.

Joy Uniforms LLP respects your privacy. This policy explains how information submitted through this website may be collected and used.

Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry, we may collect your name, organization, phone number, email address, uniform requirement and any other information you choose to provide.

How we use information

We use enquiry information to respond to you, understand your requirement, prepare proposals or quotations, coordinate samples and orders, and maintain relevant business records.

Third-party form service

The enquiry form uses FormSubmit, a third-party service, to deliver form submissions by email. Their processing is governed by their own terms and privacy practices.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers where reasonably necessary to process an enquiry or fulfil a confirmed requirement, subject to appropriate business safeguards.

Retention and security

We retain information only for as long as reasonably required for business, contractual, accounting or legal purposes. No internet transmission is completely secure, but reasonable steps should be taken to protect information under our control.

Your choices

You may request access, correction or deletion of information by emailing info@joyuniforms.co.in, subject to applicable law and record-retention obligations.
