Common questions

Useful answers before you begin.

Specific quantities, fabrics and timelines are confirmed after understanding your requirement.

Ask a Specific Question
Do you provide customized designs and branding?

Yes. Uniforms can be developed around your colours, logo, job roles and presentation standards. Branding options include embroidery and other suitable applications depending on the garment.

Can we approve a sample before bulk production?

Yes. Sampling and approval are built into the process for customized bulk requirements. The exact sampling scope is agreed at the quotation stage.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum quantities vary by garment, fabric, customization and size mix. Share your expected quantity and we will recommend the most practical route.

How long does a bulk order take?

Lead time depends on design approval, fabric availability, quantity and season. A committed timeline is shared after the requirement and sample are finalized.

Do you support repeat orders and new joiners?

Repeat-order planning can be discussed as part of the program, especially for schools and organizations with ongoing recruitment or admissions.

Which locations do you serve?

Joy Uniforms is based in Greater Noida and primarily serves Greater Noida, Noida, Delhi NCR and surrounding regions. Requirements from other locations can be evaluated based on project scope.
